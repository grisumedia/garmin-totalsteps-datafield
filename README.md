# Total Steps Data Field

[<img src="https://developer.garmin.com/static/available-badge-9e49ebfb7336ce47f8df66dfe45d28ae.svg" width="200" alt="Available on Connect IQ">](https://apps.garmin.com/en-US/apps/bd91ba4c-3f8a-460a-b122-8f8e61b8b657)

Simple data field that allows you to show your total steps of the day in any activity. May be used to know when you have reached your daily step goal during an activity.

![Preview of the data field on a Fenix 8](images/preview.png)
